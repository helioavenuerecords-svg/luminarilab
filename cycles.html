Thematic Cycle 01

Afronaut

Afronaut is the debut EP by the band AFRONAUT, released November 28th, 2025 — a body of work about love and loss, grief and frustration, set against electronic soundscapes.

The Afronaut Cycle invites artists to respond to the world of the EP through artwork in any medium — visual art, poetry, writing, and beyond. It is inspired by the real story of the Zambian Afronauts, and the cosmic, emotional universe the EP inhabits.
