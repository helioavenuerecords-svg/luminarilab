Cycle 01 · Afronaut · Poetry
Darlin.
Saw you from a galaxy away In a time in space where pain doesn't take place Floated by through the milky way and swore to take your breath away You say I'm calming so Prince Charming "Boy's too good, too dang alarming" But baby we're all instruments so tell me what your heart is strumming Darlin I would float through endless space for you Pull me closer ever closer let me melt right into you I said Darlin I would take my time Crafting every single word and making every rhyme. I take every beat and bar Cause that's my star.
