About the Artist

Kennedy Chapingiza Taonga Njobvu III — known as KC Njobvu and KC Ilgtmt — is a multi-hyphenated artist from the Copperbelt. He uses music, graphic design, poetry, and film to heal and reveal the world. His work moves across disciplines without apology, finding connection in the space between sound, image, and language.
